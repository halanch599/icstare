Registration Requirement for Presenting Authors:

  • Registration by at least one author is mandatory to present the paper and to include it in the conference proceedings.
  • Each presenting author is entitled to register and present up to two papers at the conference.
  • If a registered author wishes to present more than two papers, we require the author to register independently for each additional paper beyond the initial two.
