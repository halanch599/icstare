Call for Sponsorship

For more information for sponsorship opportunities please email info@ccidsa.com. In addition, provide details about your organization and sponsorship package you are interested in.

We offer a variety of sponsorship packages to suit your needs and budget. Your financial support will ensure that the costs of the conference remain accessible for students, and will improve the quality of the conference experience for all attendees worldwide. As a sponsor, you will receive benefits such as prominent branding on conference materials, speaking opportunities, and even host special sessions.

Different type of Sponsorship Packages are as follows:

Platinum Sponsorship: USD 6000

  • Prominent branding with organization's logo in the conference program.
  • Prominent branding with organization's logo in the conference website.
  • Official hyperlink of sponsor’s home page on website.
  • Banner prominently displayed during the entire Conference.
  • Keynote in the opening plenary and invited talk in one conference session.
  • Opportunity to host a workshop or special session during the conference
  • Possiblity to submit a 2 minute video to be played before technical sessions
  • Sponsor Profile with LNCS Conference Proceedings
  • Branding sponsor logo in all rooms
  • Sponsor Information included in Newsletter

Gold Sponsorship: USD 4000

  • Prominent branding with organization's logo in the conference program.
  • Prominent branding with organization's logo in the conference website.
  • Official hyperlink of sponsor’s home page on website.
  • Option to speak to the audience at their hosted event.
  • Sponsor Information included in Newsletter
  • Sponsor Profile with LNCS Conference Proceedings
  • Branding sponsor logo in all rooms

Silver Sponsorship: USD 2000

  • Prominent branding with organization's logo in the conference program.
  • Prominent branding with organization's logo in the conference website.
  • Official hyperlink of sponsor’s home page on website.
  • Branding sponsor logo in all rooms
